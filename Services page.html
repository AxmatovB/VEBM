Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our standard operating hours are Monday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Elite members have 24/7 access.

Do you offer any trial periods?

Yes, we offer a 7-day trial period for new members. This gives you the opportunity to explore our facilities and try our services before committing to a membership.

Can I freeze my membership if needed?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year due to illness, injury, or extended travel. A small maintenance fee may apply during the freezing period.

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

We provide all the necessary fitness equipment for your workouts. You only need to bring appropriate workout attire, athletic shoes, and a water bottle. Towel service is available for Elite members.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer free parking for all our members in our dedicated parking lot. The parking area is well-lit and monitored for your safety.
